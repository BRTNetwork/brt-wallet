Support

If you experience any issues with the BRT Wallet, there are multiple ways to contact us:

Reddit: Join our official subreddit and post your question here. Github: Report an error or bug on the BRTNetwork GitHub. FAQ: Go to the brt FAQ page on the website. The answer may already be there. Email: Feel free to get support by email at support@brt.network. Social media: Follow us on Facebook or Twitter.
