Swap your old casinocoins to new coins by initiating a swap. As soon as you send an amount to the deposit address the swap will be started. A minimal confirmation time of 200 blocks is needed and it can take up to 24 hours to receive your new coins.

Do not sent coins multiple times to the same swap deposit address but rather create a new one.

Right-Click on a swap to copy the Deposit Address or Swap ID.
